Welcome to the International Art Museum of Brand Peavy, home to be, becoming, and being,
with an abstract intention on stretching the human-being into the history of melanated carbon footprints
and consciously reaching for the change of tomorrow. IAMBP galleries combine culture and identity from
countries to villages intended to remind one to remember to look how far we've come.
We welcome you to IAMBP and hope you feel your way home. Enjoy!
